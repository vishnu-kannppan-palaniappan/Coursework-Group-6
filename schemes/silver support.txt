## Silver Support Scheme

**Objective**  
Provide quarterly cash support to seniors with lower lifetime earnings and limited current income.

**Eligibility**  
- Singapore Citizen  
- Aged **65 and above**  
- **Lifetime CPF contributions ≤ $140,000** (or average trade income ≤ $27,600 for self-employed)  
- Living in **1- to 5-room HDB**  
- **Household monthly income per person ≤ $2,300**

**Benefits (per quarter)**  
- **1–2 room:** up to **$1,080** (higher band) / **$540** (lower band)  
- **3-room:** up to **$860** / **$430**  
- **4-room:** up to **$650** / **$325**  
- **5-room (tenant):** up to **$430** / **$215**
