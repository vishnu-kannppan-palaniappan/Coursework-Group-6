
##Gst Voucher system

**Objective
-Offset the impact of GST and rising cost of living
-Provide targeted support to lower- and middle-income Singaporeans
-Ensure GST remains progressive

**Eligibility
-Singapore Citizen
-Aged 21 and above
-Annual income up to about $34,000
-Lives in a property with Annual Value up to $21,000
-Owns no more than one property

**Benefits
-Cash payout: about $450 to $850 per year, depending on income and property value
-U-Save rebates: about $440 to $760 per year to offset utility bills (credited to utilities account)
-MediSave top-ups: about $150 to $450 per year for seniors aged 65 and above
