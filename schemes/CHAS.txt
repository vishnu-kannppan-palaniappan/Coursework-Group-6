##CHAS (Community Health Assist Scheme)

**Objective
-Reduce out-of-pocket healthcare costs
-Ensure affordable access to primary healthcare
-Support lower- and middle-income Singaporeans, especially those with chronic illnesses

**Eligibility
-Singapore Citizen
-Household monthly income per person up to about $2,300 OR property Annual Value up to $21,000
-Tiered into CHAS Blue, Orange, or Green
-Administered by the Ministry of Health

**Benefits

**GP consultation subsidies:
-CHAS Blue: about $18–$21 per visit
-CHAS Orange: about $10–$13 per visit
-CHAS Green: about $6–$8 per visit

**Chronic disease subsidies (e.g. diabetes, hypertension):
-CHAS Blue: up to $80–$125 per visit
-CHAS Orange: up to $50–$80 per visit
-CHAS Green: up to $30–$50 per visit

**Dental subsidies:
-CHAS Blue: up to $260–$290 per year
-CHAS Orange: up to $170–$220 per year
-CHAS Green:No subsidised rate
